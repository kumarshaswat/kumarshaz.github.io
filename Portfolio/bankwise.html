Bankwise

My first hackathon will be my most memorable one because we won! I had the fantastic opportunity to participate in HackUTD IX, one of the largest hackathons in the USA, with my amazing teammates Abel Thomas, Abhishek Madhavan, and Manasi Vipat.

In 24 hours, our team developed an app called Bankwise, a banking portfolio that allocates multiple banking accounts from different banks all into one single app. From checking your credit cards to managing finances to viewing your statements every month, Bankwise is the innovation for online in-app banking! We were so proud to have gotten this far with our first hackathon, and even prouder that our team won first place in the Design Track challenge!

I believe that I learned a lot during this experience, met so many companies and like-minded people, got to apply my skills to numerous challenges, and certainly pushed myself to my limits. However, I still had a great time working with my team, presenting our project, and seeing other projects innovating upon the many challenges.

I would like to again thank my amazing team, the HackUTD team, and the sponsors we presented to for a fantastic weekend and a wonderful experience!
